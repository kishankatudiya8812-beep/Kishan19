About Our Project

Fraud Detection for UPI Transactions is a cybersecurity awareness project that educates users about secure digital payments and common online frauds.

Project Overview

This website helps users understand different types of UPI fraud, their causes, warning signs, and preventive measures. It also provides basic guidance on recognizing suspicious payment requests and fake QR codes.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create awareness among digital payment users and reduce financial fraud by promoting safe online transaction practices.

Project Objectives

  • Create awareness about UPI transaction fraud.
  • Educate users about cyber threats and phishing attacks.
  • Explain how to identify fake UPI payment requests.
  • Promote safe and secure digital payment practices.
  • Reduce financial losses through user awareness.

Key Features

🔒 Secure Payment Tips

Learn how to make secure UPI transactions and avoid fraud.

📱 Fake QR Detection

Understand how fake QR codes are used by scammers.

⚠ Fraud Awareness

Know the latest online fraud techniques and stay alert.

💡 Safety Guide

Simple cybersecurity tips for every UPI user.

Advantages

  • Supports cybersecurity education for students and the public.

Conclusion

Digital payments have become an essential part of everyday life, but they also bring security risks. This project spreads awareness about UPI frauds and encourages users to verify payment requests, protect their UPI PIN, and follow safe online practices. Staying informed is the best defense against cyber fraud.
