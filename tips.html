UPI Safety Tips

Follow these tips to stay safe from online fraud and scams.

🔒 Never Share UPI PIN

Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone, even if they claim to be bank officials.

📱 Verify Before Payment

Always check UPI ID, name and amount before sending money.

⚠ Avoid Unknown Links

Do not click on unknown SMS, WhatsApp or email links.

📷 Check QR Codes

Always verify QR codes before scanning them for payment.

🚨 Report Fraud

If you suspect fraud, immediately report it to your bank or cyber cell.
